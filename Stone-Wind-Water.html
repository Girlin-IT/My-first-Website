Sanemi Shinazugawa - THE WIND PILLAR


He is the current Wind Hashira and uses the Wind Breathing.
He is abrasive, hot-blooded, extroverted, stubborn and impulsive. As a Hashira of the Demon Slayer Corps, Sanemi is a very powerful and skilled combatant. He has displayed his extraordinary abilities and proficiency in combat on multiple occasions. He and Gyomei are highly skilled even among the ranks of the Hashira. He also possesses tremendous speed and reflexes. He is the second fastest runner among the Hashira. As a Demon Slayer, Sanemi carries around a standard sized and shaped Nichirin katana with a jagged pattern resembling the jaw of a beast in a shade of green. The green blade symbolizes wind. As a Hashira, Sanemi's blade has the words Destroy Demons. The design and color of the blade resemble Sanemi's fighting style, which is replete with movements that are extremely fast and powerful.

Giyu always wears a stoic and unbothered expression on his face. However, he has a reserved personality and a very strong sense of justice.
