The Hashira or Pillars are the 9 highest ranking combatants, most powerful and skilled members of the Demon slayer corps. As such, they are the organization's most elite and powerful warriors. They each serve a pivotal role in their war against Muzan Kibutsuji (Demon King) and the Twelve Kizuki (demon moons). They use a Breathing Style Technique that helps them develop their strenght to fight an equal or superior demons.
